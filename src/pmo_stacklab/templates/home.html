PMO StackLab

PMO StackLab is an interactive, pedagogical tool for stacking astrophotography images. Every step and parameter of the pipeline is exposed so you can experiment, preview the effect of each choice, and learn how it shapes the final image.

Pipeline overview

  1. Upload — provide your light, dark, bias, and flat frames.
  2. Calibrate — remove the instrument signature.
  3. Reproject — align the frames to a common grid.
  4. Stack — integrate the frames into one image.
  5. Post-Process — stretch and finish the result.
